The best undead Empire names balance uniqueness with familiarity. While every celestial empire should have a distinct identity, the name should still feel like it belongs within the broader undead naming ecosystem.

Naming Rules for Celestial Undead Empire

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Celestial theme. Every undead empire name incorporates celestial elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the empire's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Undead naming traditions. Undead names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish undead names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Empire scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple undead empires for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.

Rule 5: Consider the name's evolution. Great names often have shortened forms, nicknames, or historical variants. A empire called "The Celestial Undead Empire" might be known colloquially as "The Celestialhold" by its inhabitants.
