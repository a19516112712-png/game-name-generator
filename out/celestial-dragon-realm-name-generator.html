Naming Rules for Celestial Dragon Realm

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Celestial theme. Every dragon realm name incorporates celestial elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the realm's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Dragon naming traditions. Dragon names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish dragon names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Realm scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.
