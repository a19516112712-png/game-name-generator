Naming Rules for Holy Titan Legion

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Holy theme. Every titan legion name incorporates holy elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the legion's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Titan naming traditions. Titan names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish titan names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Legion scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple titan legions for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.
