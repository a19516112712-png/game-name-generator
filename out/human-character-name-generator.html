Human Character Names Naming Tips

Follow these tips to create authentic, memorable human character names:

  • Embrace the human theme. Let the human element permeate every aspect of the name — from the root word to the suffix.
  • Match the character scale. A character name should convey the appropriate scope — grand for empires, tight for clans.
  • Use evocative language. Choose words that paint a picture: shadow, iron, blood, void, crystal, flame.
  • Keep it pronounceable. The most memorable names balance uniqueness with accessibility.
  • Consider the name's history. Great names hint at a deeper story — an ancient founder, a legendary battle, a sacred oath.
