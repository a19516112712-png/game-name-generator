Naming Rules for Storm Werewolf Empire

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Storm theme. Every werewolf empire name incorporates storm elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the empire's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Werewolf naming traditions. Werewolf names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish werewolf names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Empire scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple werewolf empires for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.

Rule 5: Consider the name's evolution. Great names often have shortened forms, nicknames, or historical variants. A empire called "The Storm Werewolf Empire" might be known colloquially as "The Stormhold" by its inhabitants.
