Naming Rules for Golden Vampire Order

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Golden theme. Every vampire order name incorporates golden elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the order's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Vampire naming traditions. Vampire names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish vampire names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Order scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple vampire orders for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.

Rule 5: Consider the name's evolution. Great names often have shortened forms, nicknames, or historical variants. A order called "The Golden Vampire Order" might be known colloquially as "The Goldenhold" by its inhabitants.
