Naming Rules for Dark Human Squad

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Dark theme. Every human squad name incorporates dark elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the squad's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Human naming traditions. Human names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish human names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Squad scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple human squads for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.

Rule 5: Consider the name's evolution. Great names often have shortened forms, nicknames, or historical variants. A squad called "The Dark Human Squad" might be known colloquially as "The Darkhold" by its inhabitants.
