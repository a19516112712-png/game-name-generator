Naming Rules for Golden Phoenix Squad

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Golden theme. Every phoenix squad name incorporates golden elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the squad's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Phoenix naming traditions. Phoenix names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish phoenix names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Squad scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple phoenix squads for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.
