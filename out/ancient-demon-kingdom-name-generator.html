Naming Rules for Ancient Demon Kingdom

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Ancient theme. Every demon kingdom name incorporates ancient elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the kingdom's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Demon naming traditions. Demon names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish demon names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Kingdom scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.
