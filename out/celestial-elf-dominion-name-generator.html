Naming Rules for Celestial Elf Dominion

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Celestial theme. Every elf dominion name incorporates celestial elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the dominion's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Elf naming traditions. Elf names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish elf names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Dominion scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple elf dominions for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.

Rule 5: Consider the name's evolution. Great names often have shortened forms, nicknames, or historical variants. A dominion called "The Celestial Elf Dominion" might be known colloquially as "The Celestialhold" by its inhabitants.
