Naming Rules for Infernal Phoenix Dominion

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Infernal theme. Every phoenix dominion name incorporates infernal elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the dominion's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Phoenix naming traditions. Phoenix names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish phoenix names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Dominion scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.
