Naming Rules for Arcane Phoenix Dynasty

Rule 1: The name must reflect the Arcane theme. Every phoenix dynasty name incorporates arcane elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the dynasty's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Phoenix naming traditions. Phoenix names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish phoenix names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Dynasty scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.

Rule 4: Maintain internal consistency. If you generate multiple phoenix dynastys for the same world, ensure their names share linguistic DNA while remaining distinct enough to avoid confusion.
