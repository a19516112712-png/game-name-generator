Rule 1: The name must reflect the Eternal theme. Every orc realm name incorporates eternal elements in its prefix or root word, establishing the realm's magical or elemental alignment.

Rule 2: Honor Orc naming traditions. Orc names follow specific phonetic patterns — certain consonant clusters, vowel preferences, and syllable structures that distinguish orc names from those of other races.

Rule 3: Match the Realm scale. Empire names should sound grand and sweeping; guild names should feel tight-knit and purposeful; clan names should evoke kinship and shared heritage.
